About

The Cyber Security Club is a student-run organization at City College of San Francisco.

Members and leaders are pursuing studies in cyber security, computer science, computer engineering and information systems.

Mission

The clubâ€™s main purpose is to prepare students for careers in cyber security and the tech industry. To achieve our goal, the club organizes weekly meetings where students are introduced to cyber security concepts such as: network security, digital forensics, secure coding, encryption, reverse engineering, social engineering, compliance, risk management, and business operations to name a few. In addition, the Cyber Sec will partner with CNIT instructors, local companies and organizations to bring real-world experience into the hands of students through technical workshops, soft-skills training, and job placement.
