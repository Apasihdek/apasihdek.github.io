    Our Mission

    To prepare students for careers in tech and cyber security through technical and non-technical trainings. To offer focused workshops and trainings on security concepts, soft skills, team and leadership skills. To promote diversity and inclusion. To provide access to job opportunities and more.

    Mission Statement

    The CCSF Cyber Security Club is a student-run organization at City College of San Francisco. Club member and leaders are currently pursuing studies in cyber security, computer science, computer engineering, and information systems. The clubâ€™s main purpose is to prepare students for careers in cyber security and tech industry. To achieve our goal, the club organizes weekly meetings where students are introduced to cyber security concepts such as: network security, digital forensics, secure coding, encryption, reverse engineering, and social engineering, to name a few. In addition, the Cyber Security Club will partner with CNIT instructors, local companies, and organizations to speak at club meetings and to highlight their work in cyber security.

    The Cyber Security Club supports students who compete in the intercollegiate cyber competitions such as the Western Regional Cyber Collegiate Defence Competition (WRCCDC) and Collegiate Penetration Testing Competition (CPTC) by building a Capture the Flag (CTF) practice environment. The CTF practice environment will allow advanced cyber security and computer science students the opportunity to collaborate, create, and deploy their own practice platform. The Cyber Security Club is an inclusive organization that promotes racial, gender, social, and economic diversity.
